Project Runners is to help get used shoes that are no longer in use to those that will be able to put them into good use.

Want to help people that are in need of shoes, or even a watch to run?

Help others to run by donating your used shoes/watches that are no longer in use to you.

Anything that is donated can be used by somone else and it will find its home to the right person.

If you are considering throwing away your shoes, consider that it can be used by someone else.

Have a used GPS watch that is no longer in use for you? The watch will find someone that will put it to good use. Even if it isn't a GPS watch it could still help someone time their runs.
